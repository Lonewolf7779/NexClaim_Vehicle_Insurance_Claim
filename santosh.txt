There are 7 main pages/routes that load on the UI based on your App.jsx routing:

/ — LandingPage (main entry, with actions to start claim or track)
/NexOfficer — OfficerDashboard (for officer role)
/NexSupreme — EscalationDashboard (for supreme role)
/NexSamurai — SurveyorDashboard (for samurai role)
/dashboard — CustomerDashboard (customer dashboard)
/claim — CustomerDashboard (claim form, initialAction="new")
/track — CustomerDashboard (claim tracking, initialAction="track")
There is also a catch-all route that redirects any unknown path to /.
